Who are we ?


In India , there is very less platforms which aid NGO's and people who
want to donate/volunteer collaborate together. Sahayak is a collaborative platform developed with the collaboration of Government of India and Andromeda.Co.ltd.
This platform allows donors and volunteers to find their nearby NGO's and donate directly without any hustle and with full security.





Our Work


We connect Donors all across India to the NGO's of their preferred location
or nearby areas. We make it easier for NGO's and Donors to have
direct connection through our website. We also make announcements
of different drives , camps or activities being conducted at present or in near future.
